Into the dream

‘Into the Dream’ is an installation that deals with a moving blanket as a medium to connect reality and dreams. It allows viewers to experience the sensation of entering their own dream world.

As conscious beings, we observe the moving blanket which is representing the preconscious and that links our consciousness and unconsciousness, while the shadows symbolize our unconsciousness, leading to the landscape of dreams. People can immerse themselves in their individual dream spaces and experience emotions from their subconscious mind.
