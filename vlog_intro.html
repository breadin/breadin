Vlog Intro Collection

This video is a compilation of vlog intro designs and animated title sequences, created as a personal exploration of motion graphics using Adobe After Effects and Processing. Each piece was an experiment in balancing form, typography, and rhythm— crafted with a range of imagined vlog styles in mind, from minimal aesthetics to hyperpop-inspired visuals.
